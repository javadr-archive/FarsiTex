\englishtitle{Shortest Path Problem in Triangulated Irregular Networks}
\englishauthor{Mohammad Mahdian}
\englishdegree{Bachelor of Science \\ in \\ Computer Engineering (Software)}
\englishthesisdate{November 1997}
\englishsupervisor{Dr. Mohammad Ghodsi}
\englishdepartment{Computer Engineering Department}
\begin{englishabstract}
One of the problems in the field of computational geometry and GIS, 
is the problem of finding the shortest path between two points on
a surface. In this project, we will review different algorithms for
finding the shortest path between two points on a Triangulated Irregular
Network (TIN), which is one of the models of a surface. The most
efficient known algorithm is implemented and a more efficient 
approximate scheme for this problem is introduced.
\englishkeywords{Computational Geometry, GIS, Shortest Path, Triangulated
Irregular Network (TIN)}
\end{englishabstract}
